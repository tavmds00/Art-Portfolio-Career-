    Projects
    MISSON: EL DESIERTO
    Non Resident Alien
    Museum of Modern Mars (MoMM) is a fictional production and research space, encompassing a group of researchers and creatives attempting to compose, trace and conjure various cartographies, creating architectural-ecological interventions, and ritual-performative actions. MoMM's themes surround museological and archival practices to re-think historical narratives and imagine other worlds of existence.

    MISSON: EL DESIERTO

    Museum of Modern Mars produced the first video essay in a forthcoming series for an online exhibitiox Art at a Time Like This during COIVD-19 entitled Follow The Dust. MISSION: EL DESIERTO is an essay narrative, functioning between a trailer and an episode 1. The themes focus on migration in relation to space tourism, ecology and geographic memory under the guise of a fictional essay set in the future. This exhibition was curated by Clara Bolívar
